Featured Birds Of Tamil Nadu

Sparrow

The sparrow is a small, plump bird known for its cheerful songs.

Peacock

The peacock is known for its vibrant plumage and majestic display.

Kingfisher

The kingfisher is a brightly colored bird known for its fishing skills.

Parrot

The parrot is a highly intelligent bird known for its ability to mimic sounds.

Flamingo

Flamingos get their vibrant pink color from carotenoid pigments in their diet, primarily found in algae and¬†crustaceans.

Blue Flycatcher

The blue flycatcher is known for its striking cobalt blue plumage and melodious song, typically found in forested areas across¬†Southeast¬†Asia.

Robin

The Robin is known for its bright orange-red breast and melodious song, often considered a symbol of spring in many cultures.

Prinia

Prinias are tiny, active warblers often seen flitting about in scrubby, open habitats, and are known for their distinctive, energetic¬†songs.

Indian Quail

The Indian quail is a small, ground-dwelling bird often found in grasslands and fields, known for its camouflaging plumage.

Baya Weaver

The baya weaver is famous for its intricate, hanging nests and vibrant yellow plumage during the breeding season.

House Crow

Common in urban areas, the house crow is a highly intelligent, adaptable bird known for its problem-solving abilities.

Pied Kingfisher

The pied kingfisher is a striking bird known for its black and white plumage and expert fishing skills.

Red-wattled Lapwing

This bird is known for its loud calls and distinctive red facial wattles, often found near water bodies.

Common Tailorbird

The common tailorbird is famous for its 'sewing' of leaves to create nests, often found in gardens and shrubs.

White-cheeked Barbet

Known for its resonant call, the white-cheeked barbet is often found in forests and fruit orchards.

Pheasant-tailed Jacana

This unique water bird is known for its long toes that help it walk on floating vegetation in wetlands.

Greenish Warbler

The greenish warbler is a small, migratory songbird found in forests and gardens, recognized for its soft, whistling song.

Paddyfield Pipit

This ground-dwelling bird is often seen in open fields and farmlands, with a characteristic slender body and brown streaks.

Indian Nightjar

The Indian nightjar is a nocturnal bird known for its camouflage and distinctive calls heard at dusk.

Painted Stork

The painted stork is a large, wading bird known for its pink-tipped feathers and is often found in wetlands.
